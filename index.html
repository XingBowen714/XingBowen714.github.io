Brief Biography

    I'm currently a second-year Ph.D. student at Australian Artificial Intelligence Institute in University of Technology Sydney(UTS), where I'm honored to be supervided by Prof. Ivor W. Tsang (IEEE Fellow, Director of A*STAR CFAR, previous Research Director of AAII). I got my B.Eng degree and M.Eng degree in Computer Science and Technology from Beijing Institute of Technology in 2017 and 2020.

My Research

    My research focuses natural language processing, such as sentiment analysis, dialog systems and so on. I'm especially interested in exploiting multi-task learning framework and graph structures/networks.
