Brief Biography

    Since March 2024, I have been an Associate Professor at School of Computer and Communication Engineering, University of Science and Technology Beijing (USTB).
    I obtained my Ph.D. degree from Australian Artificial Intelligence Institute (AAII), University of Technology Sydney (UTS) in February 2024, honorly supervised by Prof. Ivor W. Tsang (IEEE Fellow, Director of A*STAR CFAR, previous Research Director of AAII).
    From August 2022 to February 2024, I visited Centre for Frontier Artificial Intelligence Research (CFAR), A*STAR, Singapore, working as Prof. Tsang's research assistant.
    I got my B.Eng degree and M.Eng degree in Computer Science and Technology from Beijing Institute of Technology in 2017 and 2020, respectively.

My Research

    My research focuses on natural language processing, such as dialog systems, sentiment analysis, information extraction and so on. I'm especially interested in exploiting multi-task learning frameworks and graph structures/networks.
